Restaurante game

Game uses objects and classes to create restuarant employees and manage food inventory and 
money. 

You can hire employees or fire employees in between game rounds, also buy inventory. If you
dont have enough money to play your employees an employee will quit. 

The speed of the cooking is done asynchronously and effects your money made for that round.


UI
- Home many employees
- Amount of money 
- inventory
- list of ingredients
- food being made
